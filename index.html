GILLIAN’S COUNTRY HOUSE BED AND BREAKFAST IN GALWAY

The cosy homely atmosphere of Gillian's Bed and Breakfast in Galway is a real treat for anyone looking for a refreshing and authentic Irish experience in a unique Irish home stay. Savor the flavor of great home produced food, relax and rest in cosy bedrooms, enjoy the peace and quiet, and the wonderful landscape of Galway.

Take a break in the wide open countryside, no crowds just nature. Only a few minutes from Gillian's B&B you can find the Eyre Square Shopping Centre, two sandy beaches, spectacular coastal walks, a variety of restaurants within walking distance, friendly traditional Irish music pubs, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and a variety of water sports .

Gillian Moore your host says “I have been living in Gillian's Bed and Breakfast for the last 20 years and have enjoyed restoring this fine old house to its former glory and developing the gardens. It’s a joy to wake up each morning and take in the beautiful lush green landscape of Galway”
